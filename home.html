About Bruno Mars

Peter Gene Hernandez, more commonly known as Bruno Mars, has become one of the best-selling music artists of all time, with over 200 million records sold worldwide and 15 Grammy Awards. His ability to blend pop, R&B, funk, and soul has caused fans of all ages to fall in love with him and his music. The worldwide sensation has touched the hearts of millions across the globe with songs that are still as loved today as they were when first released.

Facts about Bruno Mars

A photo of Bruno Mars on stage singing at his sold-out concert at the opening of Intuit Dome in Los Angeles.

Some facts about Bruno Mars:

  • Birthday: October 8, 1985
  • Born in: Honolulu, Hawaii
  • First artist to have six songs reach 7 million+ copies sold in the US
  • Started his career as a child impersonating Elvis Presley in Honolulu
  • "Uptown Funk" spent 14 weeks at #1
